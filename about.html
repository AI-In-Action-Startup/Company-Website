Our Story

Founded in 2023, AgentixAI emerged from a vision to revolutionize how businesses operate by harnessing the power of agentic AI. Our founders, a team of AI researchers and enterprise software veterans, recognized that while generative AI was making headlines, the real potential lay in autonomous AI agents that could understand, learn, and act independently.

We started with a simple mission: to build AI agents that solve real business problems. We assembled a team of experts in machine learning, natural language processing, and enterprise software development to create a new generation of AI solutions.

Today, AgentixAI is at the forefront of the agentic AI revolution, with offices in San Francisco and Gurgaon, serving clients across North America, Europe, and Asia.
