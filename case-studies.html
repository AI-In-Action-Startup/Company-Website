Finance & Banking

Global Bank SRE Transformation

Client: Fortune 500 Global Financial Institution

Challenge

The client's IT operations team was struggling with a high volume of incidents across their global infrastructure, leading to significant downtime and customer dissatisfaction. Their manual incident response process was slow, inconsistent, and reactive rather than proactive.

Solution

We deployed our SRE AI agents to monitor the client's infrastructure, detect anomalies, and automatically diagnose and resolve incidents. The solution included:

  • Real-time monitoring of over 50,000 infrastructure components
  • Automated incident triage and categorization
  • Self-healing scripts for common issues
  • Predictive analytics to identify potential failures before they occur
